About Me

I'm a dedicated Embedded Software Engineer at Infineon Technologies, developing robust driver libraries and device configuration tooling for modern microcontrollers.

Pursuing my Master's in Automotive Software Engineering at TU Chemnitz, I operate at the intersection of hardware constraints and software architecture — where every byte of RAM matters and every CPU cycle counts.

Beyond the automotive domain, I document everything I learn — I maintain dedicated repositories for VHDL and TCL where I write lessons, introductory notes, and automation scripts. My current focus is shifting towards the Internet of Things (IoT), bridging the gap between embedded hardware and connected ecosystems.
